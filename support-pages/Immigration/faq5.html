What happens after an immigration medical exam?

After an immigration medical exam, the immigration physician will complete and sign the Form I-693 report and seal it in an envelope.

This sealed envelope can either be mailed directly to the consulate or taken there personally. It is crucial to ensure that the envelope remains sealed, unopened, and undamaged. Any tampering, opening, or damage to the envelope will result in immediate rejection.
