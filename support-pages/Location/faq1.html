Locations and Working Hours

Hicksville

  • Monday – Friday: 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM (Meal break: 1:30 PM – 2:00 PM)
  • Saturday and Sunday: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM (No meal break)
  • Landmarks: Located between Newbridge Road and Old Country Road in the CVS shopping center.
  • Parking: Available
  • By Train: LIRR to Hicksville Station. Walk south or take a taxi.

Astoria

  • Monday – Friday: 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM (Meal break: 1:30 PM – 2:00 PM)
  • Saturday and Sunday: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM (No meal break)
  • Landmarks: Corner of 23rd Ave and 38th Street, near the Deli.
  • Parking: Street parking only
  • By Subway: Take N or W trains to the last stop, Ditmars Blvd.

Bartow, Bronx

  • Monday – Friday: 9:00 AM – 9:00 PM (Meal break: 2:00 PM – 2:30 PM)
  • Saturday and Sunday: 9:00 AM – 3:00 PM
  • Landmarks: Bartow Mall, near Dollar Tree and Arby’s between Asch Loop and Bartow Ave.
  • Parking: Available.
  • By Bus: Accessible via Bx23, Bx26, and several other Bronx buses.

174th Street, Bronx

  • Monday – Friday: 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM (Meal break: 1:30 PM – 2:00 PM)
  • Saturday: 9:00 AM – 3:00 PM
  • Sunday: Closed
  • Landmarks: Near Southern Blvd and Hoe Avenue.
  • Parking: Street parking only

Brooklyn

  • Monday – Friday: 8:00 AM – 9:00 PM (Meal break: 1:30 PM – 2:00 PM)
  • Saturday and Sunday: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM
  • Landmarks: Near Franklin Ave and Eastern Parkway above the Franklin Ave subway station.
  • Parking: Street parking only

Holiday Hours

All locations are open on holidays from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM. Please contact your nearest clinic for location-specific holiday schedules.
