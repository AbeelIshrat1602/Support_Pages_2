How can I avoid unexpected urgent care bills?

While Nao Medical strives to provide transparent and affordable healthcare, unexpected bills can still occur. Here are some tips to minimize these risks:

Before Your Visit:

  • Check Your Insurance: Ensure Nao Medical is in-network with your insurance provider. Understand your co-pays, deductibles, and out-of-pocket maximums.
  • Use the Nao Medical App or Website: Check for real-time pricing information on specific services and use the app to estimate potential costs based on your insurance plan.

During Your Visit:

  • Ask Questions: Donâ€™t hesitate to ask about the cost of any tests, procedures, or medications. Inquire about any potential additional charges that may arise.
  • Verify Insurance Information: Double-check that your insurance information is accurate and up-to-date.

After Your Visit:

  • Review Your Bill: Carefully examine your bill for any errors or unexpected charges. If you have questions, contact Nao Medical's billing department.
  • Consider Financial Assistance: If you're facing financial difficulties, inquire about payment plans or financial assistance programs.

Additional Tips:

  • Preventive Care: Regular check-ups and preventative care can help avoid urgent care visits.
  • Telehealth: For non-urgent issues, consider telehealth consultations as a more affordable option.
