What is the cost of a walk-in urgent care visit without insurance?

At Nao Medical, we believe in providing affordable and transparent care. Your cost for a walk-in urgent care visit without insurance is $175.

We accept all insurances, ensuring that you don’t face unexpected costs. If you don’t have insurance, you can still access quality care at an accessible price. Don’t overpay for urgent care.
