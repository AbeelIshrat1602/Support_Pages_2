Is it covered by my insurance?

Nao Medical is a certified urgent care clinic that accepts most major insurance plans. Even if you don’t have insurance, we accept out-of-pocket payments with transparent pricing. There’s no need for a referral from your primary care physician (PCP); we’re in-network as an urgent care center for numerous insurance plans.

Copays & Deductibles: Avoid Sticker Shock

Here's what you need to know about your costs:

Care Without Insurance:

Your cost for walk-in urgent care at Nao Medical is $175 without health insurance. By comparison, most other walk-in urgent care facilities accredited by the Urgent Care Association charge more than $280 in the New York area.

Care With Health Insurance:

If you have health insurance and need immediate care, it’s essential to understand how your plan works, as healthcare transparency is often limited. While urgent care saves costs compared to emergency rooms, choosing the right facility can help avoid unexpected expenses.

Why Choose Urgent Care Over ER for Non-Life-Threatening Issues

Emergency Room (ER) visits can cost over $1,200 on average and are a costly option for non-life-threatening situations. With over 10,000 urgent care centers in the US, far exceeding ERs, urgent care facilities like Nao Medical offer affordable, immediate medical attention without financial pitfalls.
